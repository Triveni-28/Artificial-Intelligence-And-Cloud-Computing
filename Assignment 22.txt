Word Pairs and Similarity.
Word Pair	            Similarity       Explanation
Car – Automobile	      High	         They have the same meaning (synonyms).
Happy – Joyful	          High	         They express similar emotions.
Teacher – Student	      Medium	     They are related but have different roles.
Dog – Animal	          Medium	     One is a category of the other.
Hot – Cold	              Low	         They are opposites.
    
Explanation.

Semantic similarity refers to how close the meanings of two words are.

Words can be grouped based on their level of similarity:

Synonyms have high similarity because they mean the same thing.
Related words have medium similarity because they are connected but not identical.
Opposite words have low similarity because their meanings are completely different.
