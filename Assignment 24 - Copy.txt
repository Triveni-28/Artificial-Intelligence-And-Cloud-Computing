Weak vs Strong Prompts.
1. Resume.
Weak Prompt: Write a resume.
Strong Prompt: Write a professional resume for a computer science student skilled in Python, machine learning, and data analysis. Include projects and internship experience.
2. Business Idea.
Weak Prompt: Give a business idea.
Strong Prompt: Suggest a low-budget business idea for a college student in India related to AI or online services, including cost and profit potential.
3. Study Plan.
Weak Prompt: Make a study plan.
Strong Prompt: Create a 4-week study plan to learn machine learning basics, including Python, regression, classification, and mini projects with a daily schedule.
Comparison Table.
Feature	Weak Prompt	Strong Prompt
Clarity	Low	High
Details	Missing	Specific
Output Quality	Basic	Detailed
Control	Less	More
Conclusion.

Strong prompts produce better and more useful results.

A good prompt should include:

Context.
Goal.
Constraints.
Details.
